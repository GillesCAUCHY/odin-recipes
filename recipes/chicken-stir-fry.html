Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

A Flavorful Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

Chicken stir-fry is a vibrant and quick dish packed with colorful vegetables and tender chicken, all coated in a savory sauce. Start by slicing boneless chicken breast into thin strips and marinating them in soy sauce and cornstarch for 10 minutes to enhance flavor and texture. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat, then cook the chicken until browned and set aside. In the same pan, stir-fry minced garlic, sliced onion, bell pepper, and broccoli florets until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

Return the chicken to the pan and pour in your favorite stir-fry sauce, tossing everything together to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2–3 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened slightly. Drizzle with sesame oil for extra aroma, if desired. Serve immediately over steamed rice or noodles for a satisfying meal that’s ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 tbsp stir-fry sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Steps to achieve our Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Cook chicken until browned, then set aside.
  4. Stir-fry garlic, onion, bell pepper, and broccoli for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Return chicken to the pan.
  6. Add stir-fry sauce and toss to coat.
  7. Cook for 2 more minutes until heated through.
  8. Drizzle with sesame oil (optional).
  9. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
