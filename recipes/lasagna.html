Lasagna Recipe

A simple yet enjoyable Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish known for its rich layers of flavor and comforting texture. To start, prepare a hearty meat sauce by browning ground beef or a mix of beef and pork in a pan with olive oil, garlic, and onions. Add tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and a blend of Italian herbs like oregano and basil, then let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes to deepen the flavors. While the sauce cooks, boil lasagna noodles until al dente, or use no-boil noodles for convenience. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan, and a pinch of salt and pepper to create a creamy filling.

To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, a generous spread of the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of noodles topped with meat sauce and mozzarella. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25–30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the layers to set, then enjoy with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (or no-boil noodles)
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 cup (250g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to achieve our Lasagna Recipe

  1. Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes, paste, and herbs; simmer 20 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles (or use no-boil).
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a dish.
  6. Add a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Spread half the ricotta mixture over noodles.
  8. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella.
  10. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes.
